The Best Movies according to Prajwal!!

My Top 3 Movies of all time!!

1. Interstellar

When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future,
a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked
to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers,
to find a new planet for humans.

2. Mission Impossible: Ghost Protocol

The IMF is shut down when it's implicated in the bombing
of the Kremlin, causing Ethan Hunt and his new team to go rogue
to clear their organization's name.

3. The Dark Knight

When a menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on
the people of Gotham, Batman, James Gordon and Harvey Dent must
work together to put an end to the madness.
